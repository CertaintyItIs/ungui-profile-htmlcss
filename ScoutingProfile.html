I am Eagle Scout Isaac Thomas Ungui of Outfit 02 Eagles' Nest of Capiz National High School. I am the former Senior Crew Leader for 2 years and the Outfit Cheerleader for a year on the same outfit. My Specialist Ratings are Scout Scientist and Scout Emergencyman.
